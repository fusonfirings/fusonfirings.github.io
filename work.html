My Gallery Work

This collection showcases pieces that I consider to be my portfolio, or were created with certain shows or events in mind. Each one reflects my love for form, texture, and the beauty of handmade ceramics. This collection holds the pieces where I’ve taken my time—where each curve, edge, and surface has been shaped with extra intention. These are the forms I return to, refine, and quietly push a little further.

My Production Work

This collection features pieces made with the intention of sharing my work more widely—ceramics crafted to find new homes. Created after graduation, these works mark the beginning of my journey into building a business and discovering a style that feels like my own. Each piece is meant to be both functional and expressive—art for the kitchen, made to bring small moments of joy to everyday use.
